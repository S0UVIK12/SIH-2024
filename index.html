Prime Minister's Special Scholarship Scheme

Prime Minister's Special Scholarship Scheme (PMSSS) is a scholarship scheme that was launched in 2011-12. The scheme is aimed at providing financial assistance to pursue higher education. The scheme is funded by the Ministry of Human Resource Development, Government of India. The scheme is implemented by the All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE).

The scheme is open to students who have passed the Class 12 examination. The students who are eligible for the scheme can apply for the scholarship through the online portal of the AICTE. The scholarship is awarded to the students on the basis of their performance in the qualifying examination and the entrance examination conducted by the AICTE.

Frequently Asked Questions

The Prime Minister's Special Scholarship Scheme (PMSSS) is a scheme aimed at providing financial assistance to students from specific regions in India to pursue higher education. The scholarship is awarded based on performance in qualifying exams and entrance exams.

Students who have passed the Class 12 examination from recognized schools or institutions in specific regions of India are eligible for PMSSS. Eligibility is also based on family income and other criteria.

Eligible students can apply for PMSSS through the AICTE online portal. The application process involves filling out necessary details, submitting required documents, and appearing for entrance exams if applicable.
